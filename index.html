Why partner with us?

Possible activities: online projects, penpals, virtual student meetings, eTwinning collaborations, shared blogs, communication challenges etc. If possible we'd love to see this expand into a real-life exchange.

About our school

We are College Den Hulster VWO Bilingual, a pre-university school in Venlo, Netherlands, right near the German border. We offer full bilingual VWO education with many subjects taught in English. Our motivated students are used to working in English, using digital tools, and collaborating internationally. They love intercultural projects and are open to new cultures and ideas. This makes them perfect partners for global communication and teamwork!
