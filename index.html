College Den Hulster VWO Bilingual

Connect Globally Through Communication

We are seeking enthusiastic partner schools worldwide for an international VWO bilingual project focused on **intercultural & digital communication** skills.

Why partner with us?

Our VWO bilingual program delivers advanced English proficiency and a strong international outlook. Students excel in real-world communication: debating global issues, creating digital content, bridging cultural gaps, and collaborating across borders.

Possible activities: online projects, penpals, virtual student meetings, eTwinning collaborations, shared blogs, communication challenges etc. If possible we'd love to see this expand into a real-life exchange.

Project Ideas – Communication in Action

Intercultural debates & global issue discussions
Collaborative podcasts or YouTube-style videos
Digital storytelling & multilingual campaigns
Virtual language cafés & student meetups
Media literacy: fake news & social media impact
Public speaking challenges across cultures
eTwinning twinspaces with shared outputs
Erasmus+ virtual mobility & small partnerships

English Version

We are a pre-university bilingual school (VWO bilingual) in Venlo, Netherlands. Motivated students aged 15–18 are ready for authentic international teamwork. This project strengthens language, cultural awareness, and 21st-century skills like digital collaboration and critical thinking.

Nederlandse versie

College Den Hulster VWO bilingual biedt onderwijs in het Engels op vwo-niveau: veel lessen in het Engels, focus op taal, cultuur en internationale competenties. We zoeken partners voor een communicatie-project: intercultureel contact, digitale media, wereldwijd debatteren en samenwerken.
