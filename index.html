College Den Hulster (Venlo, Netherlands) – VWO TTO (Tweetalig Onderwijs Engels) – is looking for partner schools from other countries for an exciting international school project focused on **communication**.

English version

In our bilingual VWO program (TTO), students develop advanced English skills and an international mindset. We want to collaborate on a project centered around **communication**: intercultural dialogue, digital media & messaging, effective global exchange, breaking down language barriers, social media impact, public speaking across cultures, or building understanding through stories and debates.

Possible activities: online projects, penpals, virtual student meetings, eTwinning collaborations shared blogs, communication challenges etc. If possible we'd love to see this expand into a real-life exchange.

Are you a secondary school (high school) interested in improving communication skills through international teamwork? We'd love to partner with you!

Nederlandse versie

Op ons VWO TTO (tweetalig Engels) richten we ons sterk op taal- en communicatievaardigheden. We zoeken partner-scholen uit andere landen voor een project over **communicatie**: interculturele uitwisseling, digitale media, effectief wereldwijd contact, taalbarrières overwinnen, sociale media, debatteren of verhalen delen over culturen.

Ideeën: eTwinning-projecten, Erasmus+ partnerschappen, online meetings, gezamenlijke podcasts/video's, blogs, communicatie-opdrachten of meertalige creatieve outputs.

Interesse? Neem contact op voor een samenwerking!
